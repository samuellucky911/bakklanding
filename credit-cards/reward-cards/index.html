Collect rewards or cashback with your everyday spending

Earn rewards when you spend on Amazon and beyond1, collect travel rewards with Barclaycard Avios2 or get cashback on eligible purchases with Barclaycard Rewards. 80.1% APR Representative (variable).

The Amazon Barclaycard

  • Apply before 11:59pm on 8 December and – if accepted – you'll get a £30 Amazon Gift Card (usually £20), added to the Amazon account you applied from
  • Get 1% back in rewards when you shop on Amazon1 and 0.5% back everywhere else3. Amazon Prime members get 2% back in rewards during eligible Amazon key shopping events, like Prime Day4
  • Earn £5 in rewards then transfer a £5 Amazon Gift Card to your linked Amazon.co.uk account – or let them keep growing
  • Get 0% interest on all purchases for the first 6 months from the date you open your account5
  • This is an app-only account. You’ll need to use the Barclays or Barclaycard app to manage your account6.

Amazon Barclaycard Rewards Rules [PDF, 148KB] apply to the features of this card.

Unfortunately, Apple Pay is unavailable for the Amazon Barclaycard. You can still use your card with contactless, or on Google Pay if you have an Android device.

Barclaycard Avios Plus

  • If you’re new to Barclaycard, you'll collect 25,000 Avios if you spend £3,000 in your first three months. T&Cs apply.
  • Collect 1.5 Avios for every £1 you spend on eligible purchases, for a £20 monthly fee
  • Spend £10,000 on your card within 12 months and choose between a British Airways cabin upgrade voucher to use on an Avios Reward Flight booking, or 7,000 bonus Avios. T&Cs apply
  • Airport lounge membership – access to over 1,000 airport lounges worldwide at a discounted rate of £18.50 per lounge pass, per person
  • Spend everywhere that accepts Mastercard and keep collecting Avios
