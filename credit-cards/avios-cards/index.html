Choose your Barclaycard Avios credit card

With a Barclaycard Avios credit card you can unlock exciting new travel experiences and enhance your holiday plans.

Whichever card you choose, you’ll collect Avios with every £1 you spend on eligible purchases1. Avios is the reward currency of the British Airways Executive Club2. Avios can help you design your dream getaway by spending them on flights, cabin upgrades, hotel stays, car hire and more.

With two cards to choose from, it’s even easier to get closer to the people and places that matter most with your everyday spending. You could even collect a welcome bonus to kick start your travel plans and a cabin upgrade voucher (T&Cs apply). You can watch your rewards grow in the Barclays app5.

And if you don’t have a British Airways Executive Club account, we’ll set one one up for you when you apply for your chosen Barclaycard Avios card. We’ll transfer the Avios you collect with us to that account.

Barclays Avios Rewards

Enjoy flights, hotels and more with Barclaycard and Avios

Avios is the reward currency of the British Airways Executive Club2. You can spend your Avios for rewards such as flights, upgrades, hotel stays, car hire and more.

Adding together your Avios for everyday spending and your welcome bonus, you’d collect 25,000 Avios if you spent £20,000 on the card in your first year. You’d also receive a British Airways cabin upgrade voucher to use on an Avios Reward Flight booking*. See ‘About collecting Avios and rewards’ below for details.

You can spend your Avios on all kinds of travel experiences. Here are some examples of where your Avios could take you.

19,500 Avios + £1 for a peak, economy return flight from London to Milan*

24,000 Avios + £9 for a peak, economy return flight from London to Barcelona **

Visit ba.com to learn how to use your Avios and for more information about Reward Flight Saver.

About collecting Avios and rewards

  • You’ll collect Avios on new purchases, but not on balance transfers, money transfers and transactions that make up your cash balance. These include cash withdrawals, wire transfers and buying currency. You won’t collect Avios for any transactions that are later returned or refunded. You also need to keep to the terms and conditions of your account and stay within your credit limit. You can find out more about collecting and spending Avios, including which purchases are eligible. 

  • When you apply for a Barclaycard Avios card, you can either link it to an existing British Airways Executive Club membership or we’ll set one up for you. Then we’ll transfer the Avios you collect with us to your British Airways Executive Club account each month. You can check your latest Avios balance in your Barclaycard or Barclays app5. Take a look at the terms and conditions of the British Airways Executive Club and check the British Airways privacy policy before you apply.

  • Cabin upgrade vouchers must be used on Avios reward flight bookings. Reward flights and cabin upgrades are subject to availability. Taxes, fees and carrier charges per person apply. You can read the additional terms and conditions that apply to using the cabin upgrade voucher.

  • Reward flights are subject to availability. To be eligible for Reward Flight Saver, you need to have collected at least 1 Avios in the past 12 months. Other pricing options are available. Visit ba.com to learn how to use your Avios and for more information about Reward Flight Saver.

  • You need to be 18 or over to access this product or service using the app. Terms and conditions apply. 

  • You can opt in to Barclays Avios Rewards if you’re a Premier Banking customer or have a personal Wealth current account with us – you’ll need to register for the Barclays app and have a British Airways Executive Club account too. There’s a £12 monthly fee and you have to opt out of Barclays Blue Rewards and go paperless for all your accounts and services with us. To access Barclays Avios Rewards in the Barclays app, you need to be over 18. You can join Premier Banking if you have an income of £75,000 or £100,000 held with us. T&Cs apply.

    • Barclaycard Avios card – 1,000 Avios when you spend at least £1,000 within three months of receiving the card
    • Barclaycard Avios Plus card – 5,000 Avios if you spend at least £3,000 within three months of receiving your card

    You can use the Barclays or Barclaycard app to check if you’re eligible to change to a different card – like one of our Barclaycard Avios cards. Log in to your app, open 'Cards' from the home page and select ‘Change to a different card’.

    You can only collect the welcome bonus once, so you won’t be eligible for it if you switch from one of these cards to the other. If you don't hit the spending target in time, you won't receive the welcome bonus. You can check how close you are to the target and how long you have left to hit it in the Barclaycard Avios section of your Barclays app.

  • We’ll transfer the Avios you collect with us to your linked British Airways Executive Club account on the second working day of the next month.

  • Yes, you will collect Avios on qualifying purchases abroad, providing you stay within your credit limit.

  • You’ll collect one cabin upgrade voucher3 for every 12-month period in which you’ve reached your Barclaycard Avios spending target. You can find more details about cabin upgrade vouchers at ba.com/cabin-upgrade-voucher.

  • A cabin upgrade voucher enables you to move up by one cabin class (where available) – from Premium Economy (World Traveller Plus) to Business (Club World), for example. Bookings cannot be upgraded to a First class cabin using the cabin upgrade voucher.

    Flights that you book using Avios are called reward flights. You can use your cabin upgrade voucher on an Avios Reward Flight booking. You can book to any worldwide destination, where there’s availability, with British Airways and its partners. You pay with your Avios plus a cash amount for taxes, fees and carrier charges per person. The cabin upgrade voucher must be used at the point of booking an Avios Reward Flight. You can’t amend an existing booking to utilise the voucher.

    The British Airways Executive Club Member needs to book the Avios Reward Flight and travel must have commenced within the 24 months following date of issue of the cabin upgrade voucher. For full terms and conditions on cabin upgrade vouchers please visit ba.com/cabin-upgrade-voucher.

You won’t be able to see it in your Barclays or Barclaycard app5. Visit ba.com/cabin-upgrade-voucher to find out more about cabin upgrade vouchers.
