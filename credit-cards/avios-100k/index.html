Avios FAQs

    The BAEC and all Avios issued and redeemed under these terms are subject to British Airways Executive Club terms and conditions.

  • Your Barclaycard can take up to 24 hours following application to show in the Barclays app. 

  • We recommend that you use the Barclays app. This will show you all of your personal banking information and your Barclaycard details.

  • To collect the bonus of 100,000 Avios, you need to hold both a Barclaycard Avios Plus credit card (£20 monthly fee) and Barclays Avios Rewards (£12 monthly fee). At least one of these products should be new and opened during the qualifying period (3 October 2022 – 30 November 2022). You’ll also need to hold the products for four months or more from the date the last product was opened. Eligibility and terms and conditions apply to each product.

  • No. Your current Barclaycard card will stop working 30 days after the transfer to your new card has been completed.

    Before your new card arrives, you can continue to use your existing card for regular and online payments as normal.
