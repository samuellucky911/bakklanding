Barclays Avios Rewards

Collect Avios now to spend on trips you’ll enjoy later

With Barclays Avios Rewards, you can start collecting Avios now to spend on future flights with British Airways.

For just £12 per month, you’ll receive the following rewards (eligibility and conditions apply1 )

1,500 Avios every month

A joining bonus and an anniversary reward 

More Avios for having or opening selected products with us

Airport Lounge Membership in partnership with Assurant and DragonPass

Plan your next trip with Barclays Avios Rewards

If you’re looking forward to planning your next holiday, discovering new destinations or reuniting with friends and family, Barclays Avios Rewards can help you get closer to the travel you want.

Collect and spend Avios

You could collect at least 20,500 Avios - enough for a peak British Airways return economy (Euro Traveller) flight to Nice by the end of your first year2. Collecting Avios helps bring you closer to the travel experiences you want, from flights and hotels to car hire and more.

Collect these rewards for just £12 a month

1,500 Avios every month

Watch your Avios balance grow in the Barclays app3 and spend them on your future trips with British Airways4.

A joining bonus

You’ll receive 25,000 Avios if you’re opening your first account with us and complete a full switch using the Current Account Switcher Service within three months5. If you’ve been with us for four months or more, you’ll receive 2,500 Avios as a joining bonus.

An anniversary reward

To celebrate every year you stay with us, you can choose between 7,000 bonus Avios or a British Airways cabin upgrade voucher, which enables you to move up by one cabin – from Premium Economy (World Traveller Plus) to Business (Club World), for example6.

You'll receive an anniversary reward for every year you've had Avios Rewards for 12 months and paid 12 monthly fees.

Airport Lounge Membership

Airport Lounge Membership lets you buy discounted passes to access airport lounges worldwide, or swap passes for a meal voucher or spa treatment. 

Escape the crowds and relax in comfort, with free refreshments and Wi-Fi.

Collect more Avios with Barclaycard

Barclaycard Avios Plus 

If you switch on Barclays Avios Rewards as a Premier customer for £12 a month and open a Barclaycard Avios Plus credit card for £20 a month, we’ll give you £5 back into your current account every month. Terms and conditions apply.

For having both, you’ll also get 4 complimentary lounge passes each year with your Airport Lounge Membership.

You can join Barclays Avios Rewards in the Barclays app. To be eligible7, you’ll need to

How to collect even more Avios

You’ll collect extra Avios when you have, or open, these products with us.

Mortgages

You’ll collect 700 monthly Avios for having a residential9 or personal buy-to-let10 mortgage with us.

If you don’t already have a residential mortgage with us, you’ll get a 2,000 Avios joining bonus when you take out a new one (including any taken out up to 30 days before joining Barclays Avios Rewards). You’ll also get a 2,000 Avios joining bonus for every new buy-to-let mortgage you take out.

You can only collect Avios for one residential mortgage, but there’s no limit to the number of personal buy-to-let mortgages you can collect Avios for.

Mortgages are subject to application, financial circumstances and borrowing history. Terms and conditions apply.

Life insurance

If you have or open life insurance without critical illness cover, you’ll receive 150 Avios a month for a maximum period of 12 months.  Or, if you have or open life insurance with critical illness cover, you’ll receive 500 Avios each month for a maximum of 12 months. The 12 months begin from the start date of the policy. This reward isn’t available to our Wealth customers.

Barclayloan

We’ll give you 200 Avios each month for each Barclayloan you have or open with us (minimum loan amount is £5,000). You’ll also receive a joining bonus of 2,000 Avios for a new Barclayloan, including any you opened up to 30 days before turning on Barclays Avios Rewards. You can only receive this joining bonus once, even if you take out more than one Barclayloan.

6.2%  APR representative on loans of £7,500 to £15,000 over 2 to 5 years.  Your rate may differ.  Loans are subject to application, financial circumstances and borrowing history.11
