Barclays offer terms and conditions

In partnership with Hugo Technologies Limited, Barclays is offering residential mortgage customers a 6 month free trial of the Hugo Pro app. 

This Offer is valid from 30 September 2024. Barclays may change, cancel or amend the Offer at any time.

If you do not cancel your membership on or before the last day of the 6 month free trial period, you will be automatically enrolled in the annual subscription plan for the Hugo Pro app, on the terms and conditions provided by Hugo Technologies Limited. All membership queries should be made with Hugo Technologies Limited directly.

The end date of your 6 month free trial period can be located within the Subscriptions section of your Hugo Pro account or alternatively confirmed by contacting the Hugo Pro support team directly.

To be eligible for this Offer you must have an existing residential mortgage with Barclays, provided via Barclays Bank UK PLC, over property in the UK.

To receive this Offer you must:

  1. Provide the last six digits of your mortgage account number, or a Barclays referral code, when signing up to the Hugo Pro app on the Hugo Pro website.
  2. Have a working smart meter in your home for the Hugo Pro app to connect to.

This Offer can only be used once per mortgage account. If you are a customer on a joint mortgage, then this Offer can only be used once as joint customers.

There is no guarantee that any of the products or services selected by you under this Offer will result in cost or energy savings or change your property’s EPC rating. Purchasing or installing any products or services under this Offer does not mean you will be able to qualify for any Barclays product now or in the future. Barclays does not endorse any of the products or services selected by you under this Offer or claims made about them. 

We recommend that you undertake research and are happy with your decision before taking any action as a result of any information or insights shared within the Hugo Pro app.

For the avoidance of doubt, Barclays will not receive a fee from Hugo Technologies Limited in connection with this Offer.
