My card’s not working

There are lots of reasons why your card might not be working online, at a cash machine or in store. Our handy tool can help you work out what the problem is, and how to fix it.

Once you’ve reported a card lost or stolen, it won’t work anymore. Have you received a new replacement card?

Was the payment for more than £100?

The limit for contactless card payments is £100. Your limit may be lower if you've changed it in the app. If you're trying to pay for something that costs more than your contactless limit, you'll need to use chip and PIN instead.

Remember, the limit is higher for smartphone payments.

From time to time you’ll need to enter your PIN instead of using contactless. Try entering your PIN for the next payment, then retry using contactless for the payment after that.

Remember, if you have set your own contactless limit and are trying to pay for something that costs more than your set limit, you’ll need to use chip and PIN instead.

Once you know your PIN, you can unlock your card at any UK cash machine by selecting ‘PIN services’ and entering the right PIN.
