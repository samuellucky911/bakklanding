Where will I see my business current accounts, held with other providers, within the Barclays app?

If you choose to add any of your UK Personal, Wealth or Business current accounts into your app, they will all be presented in one place within your Personal tab.

We’re working to further enhance the placement of Business current accounts within the app, so that they appear alongside your wider Business relationships under the Business tab.
