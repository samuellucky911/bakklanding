How do I follow my complaint in Barclays Track It?

We’ll send you a unique reference number when you make a complaint. You’ll be able to track your complaint using your reference number and see when we’ve resolved your issue. 

Have a look at our page for more on information on how to use Track It. 

We also have an online guide for how we deal with your complaint.
