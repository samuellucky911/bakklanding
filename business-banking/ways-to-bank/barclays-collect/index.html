Barclays Collect

The banking service that comes to your business

Log in to register
Check availability

We’ll collect your cash so you can focus on running your business. It’s secure and convenient, with no minimum payment or monthly fee.

  • Pay as you go and choose your collection slot with no contract or monthly fee
  • Your cash is guaranteed by G4S as soon as they take it into their care
  • This service is only available to our business and corporate clients
  • Pay in up to 50 cheques with each cash collection
  • There’s a limit on the amount of cash you can pay into your business account. You won’t be able to use Collect if you reach or go over this limit.

     

 

Barclays Collect Benefits

Safe

Items are collected by security specialist G4S from your business’ trading address, so you and your employees are safe.

Secure

Your cash is guaranteed as soon as G4S takes it into their care. It’s on CCTV at all times when it’s with us.

Convenient

Book one-off or recurring collections in the morning or afternoon. You can also book before midday for a next working day collection.

Flexible

You can use Barclays Collect when your business needs it – there’s no minimum contract or monthly fee to pay.

Paid quickly

The cash we collect will appear in your account the next working day. Cheques will appear by the second working day.

Transparent pricing

Pay-as-you-go pricing, with no fees for large deposits. There’s also no minimum usage or contract – see ‘How much does it cost?’.

How it works

  • You can make sure that Barclays Collect is available in your area by using our postcode checker. To register, you'll need:

    • A Business or Corporate current account
    • A valid email address and UK mobile phone number
    • Access to Online Banking

    If you have all of these, log in to Online Banking to register and follow the instructions. There’s no fee for signing up and you can use it as often as you like.

    Once we’ve approved your registration, you can book collections in Online Banking. For security, we may need to visit your area before we can arrange a collection.

    • The depends on how much cash you deposit. Cheque values aren’t included
    • You can deposit £20,000 in cash per collection
    • Any cash collected will appear on your account the next day and we’ll charge our usual cash and cheque processing fees. Cheques will appear on your account by the second working day.
