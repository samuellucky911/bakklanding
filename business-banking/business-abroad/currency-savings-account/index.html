    Who is the Currency Clients Premium Account suitable for?

    Currency Clients Premium Accounts are suitable for regulated businesses that have a legal requirement to hold money on behalf of their clients.

    If you’re holding money that belongs to a third party, not your own business, you can safeguard it in this account. When you open an account, you can choose between a ‘designated account’ for money belonging to a single business or person, or a ‘pooled account’ for money belonging to multiple businesses or people. If your needs change, you can still open an account of the other type.

    It’s important to remember that a Currency Clients Premium Account should never hold any of your own money.

    Our Currency Clients Premium Accounts are typically suitable for

    •  Those regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) with client money permissions

    •  Regulated insolvency practitioners, accountants and solicitors

    •  Property managers, estate agents and lettings agents

    You’re responsible for complying with all legal, fiduciary and regulatory obligations that apply to you. This includes compliance with Money Laundering Regulations.

    Can I withdraw money?

    Cash withdrawals are restricted because a Clients Premium Account is designed to safely store money on behalf of your clients. You’ll still have instant access to the money online, letting you manage it and transfer it to your clients. 

    Additional information

    • This account is specifically for separating your clients’ money from your own

    • Interest is accrued daily and paid quarterly
    • Cash deposits and cash withdrawals are restricted with this type of account
    • To hold this account, you’ll also need a sterling current account with us.
