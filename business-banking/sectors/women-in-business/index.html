Hear from Hannah


“Hi, I’m Hannah Bernard, Head of Business Banking at Barclays. I’m passionate about empowering female entrepreneurs like you to realise your ambitions.

We’re proud to be a founding signatory of the UK government’s Investing in Women code and I’m honoured to co-chair the Invest in Women Taskforce, alongside Debbie Wosskow OBE, which aims to make the UK the best place in the world to be a female entrepreneur.

We understand that women face many challenges when starting and scaling businesses – from lack of networks and resources, to navigating the complex financing landscape. That’s why we aim to provide you with the best support possible and be your genuine partner for growth.”

Inspiring success

We're here to offer expert support and funding to female-led businesses like yours to help you start-up, run and grow your business.

Boosting the economy

Nearly £250bn(1) could be added to the UK economy if women started and scaled businesses at the same rate as men.

Our commitment

Since 2019, we’ve committed to help 250,000 women start up and run their businesses.

Supporting the next generation

We’re inspiring girls to consider entrepreneurship with LifeSkills, our financial education programme available to all secondary schools in the UK.

Specialist coaching 


Our free specialist coaching sessions could help you with business planning, cashflow forecasts, digital support, accessing finance(2) and more. We offer four one-hour virtual sessions with one of our Business Relationship Managers, with flexibility to work around your schedule.

To apply, you’ll need:

  • A business account with us
  • At least one female founder
  • A UK business with an annual turnover between £50,000 and £6.5m

Fill in our callback form and we’ll match you with one of our expert relationship managers within 10 working days so you can see what coaching options are available and book in your sessions.
