How we could support your business

Discounted interest rate

You could get a loan to purchase or refinance eligible green assets (1) at discounted interest rates.

Choice of green assets

There are over 65 eligible green assets(1) for our Green Loans for Business, which you can find listed in our Eligible Green Assets Guide.

Improved energy efficiency

Depending on which asset you choose, a loan(2) could help support energy efficiency, reduce emissions, make cost savings or generate additional income.

Subject to eligibility, other product features apply as standard.  

What you could purchase or refinance

You can only take out this loan for your business if the money is used towards eligible green assets(1), like:

  • Commercial or residential property with an Energy Performance Certificate (EPC)  rating of B or above
  • Solar panels
  • Battery storage(3)
  • Electric vehicles 
  • Heat pumps 
  • Building automation and insulation 
  • Agricultural technologies.

Things you need to know before applying

  • Evidence

    You may need to provide evidence of the eligible green assets you’re planning to buy or refinance and any costs that will be associated with buying the assets , such as an invoice. 

  • Disclaimer

    Our Green Loans for Business offer a secured funding solution with discounted interest rates when purchasing or refinancing one or more eligible green assets. These are assets that meets our green eligibility criteria, as listed in our Eligible Green Assets Guide. The Eligible Green Assets Guide is informed by Barclays’ Sustainable Finance Framework, which explains our process for classifying financing as sustainable for the purpose of tracking and disclosing our performance against our sustainable finance targets. Classifying the loan as a Green Loan for Business does not mean that the loan and/or its terms and conditions align with any external sustainability related framework, principles or guidance, including the LMA Green Loan Principles.

Green asset finance

We’ve partnered with industry experts Propel to help your business get access to quick and cost-effective finance for eligible green assets(5). You could get a fixed rate  of 7.85% for an electric vehicle (EV) and 8.64% for other green assets(6).

Borrow from £5,000 and get a fixed rate for:

  • Electric vehicles (EVs)
  • Solar photovoltaic (PV) panels
  • Battery storage units(3) 
  • LED lighting
  • Heat pumps (air, water and ground)
  • EV charging points.
