Savings and investments

Your employer is enrolled in our Employer Reporting Service

To help you adhere to your firm’s reporting requirements, our reporting service automatically and securely sends all relevant account and investment information directly to your employer on your behalf.

For Smart Investor, you can set this up online once you're logged into your account by selecting ‘Manage account’ then ‘Employer Reporting Service’. You can also request this by calling us on 0800 279 36671.

Please be aware that we take no responsibility for monitoring investor activity. It's the investor's responsibility to set up reporting on all applicable accounts.
