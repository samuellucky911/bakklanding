Remortgage costs to keep in mind

Remortgage fees and costs

Understanding the remortgage costs you could face will help you decide if it’s the right time to switch. Your current lender might ask you to pay an early repayment charge or exit fee. Check your mortgage documents or contact the lender to find out.

Some lenders will cover all or part of your remortgage solicitor fees as an incentive for remortgaging with them. Be sure to check for any remortgage arrangement fees, particularly if you’re using a mortgage broker.

Your remortgage options

Choices to suit your needs

Our expert advisers will help you choose from a range of mortgage types to suit your needs. From fixed rates that give you the confidence to plan your monthly budget, to offset mortgages that put your savings to work by reducing the balance you pay interest on. You can even choose to have your mortgage appointment at a branch or by phone.
