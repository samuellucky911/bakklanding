What is an Investment (Stocks and Shares) ISA?

An Investment ISA (also known as Stocks and Shares ISA) is a tax-efficient account meaning you don’t pay UK income or capital gains tax on any returns you make. It’s a flexible way to grow your money, allowing you to invest in shares, funds, bonds and more.

Tax-efficient investment

You don’t pay UK income tax or capital gains tax on any returns you make from your investments in a Stocks and Shares ISA.

Trusted by millions

Over 37m Stocks and Shares ISAs have been used since 2008 with the aim to grow your money tax-free.

Flexible withdrawals

You can withdraw cash at any time and top back up before the end of the tax year without affecting your annual allowance.

Wide investment choice

ISAs can be invested in UK and overseas shares, bonds, funds, Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs) and investment trusts.

The value of investments can fall as well as rise so you may get back less than you invest. Tax rules can change and their effects vary depending on your individual circumstances. Please check the costs involved before transferring.
