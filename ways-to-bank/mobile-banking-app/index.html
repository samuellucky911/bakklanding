The Barclays app

Join 10 million Barclays customers using our app

Use your phone or tablet to manage your accounts, send money and earn rewards(1)

Download the Barclays app

The quickest way to open a new bank account is using our app. Scan the QR code using the camera on your mobile device to download the Barclays app.

If you’re using your mobile device now, download it from your app store below.

Manage your money with the Barclays app

Pay and transfer money

Use the app to make secure payments and transfer money between your accounts. You can also add your cards to Apple Pay and Google Wallet.

Moving money

See your spending 

Get personalised insights on your spending to help you find ways to save.

Spending breakdown

Stay in control of your card

You can set limits for different payment types, get a reminder of your PIN, and temporarily freeze your card if it’s been lost or stolen .

Managing your debit card

Send us a message

Send us a message straight from the ‘Help’ section of our app if you have a question. You don’t have to wait for our reply, just log back in when you’re ready to see if we've replied.

More reasons to use the app

Secure banking

We use the latest security features to keep your money safe.

Pay in a cheque

Pay in cheques without having to come to us – our app can use your device's camera to take a picture and we’ll take care of the rest.
