The highest standards of security and control

Safe and secure

Adding current accounts to your Barclays app from other banks is safe and secure – we’ll never ask you to reveal your log-in details for your other banks. You can choose which accounts to add, and after 90 days we’ll ask you to give your permission again, keeping you in control.

You're always in control

It’s safe and secure to add other accounts to your Barclays app because we’ll never ask you to reveal your log-in details for other banks. You choose which accounts to add and, after 90 days, we’ll ask for your permission again, keeping you in control.

If you change your mind, it’s easy to stop linking other accounts to your app. Just go to ‘Settings’ then ‘Manage third-party accounts’.

A safe way to pay

Moving money from your accounts with other banks using your Barclays app is safe and secure. To make a payment from a linked account, go to ‘Pay & Transfer’ and follow the instructions, as you would for your account with us. Once you’ve made the payment, your other bank will ask you to authorise it – keeping you in control of your money every step of the way.
